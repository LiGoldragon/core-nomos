## Resolved

### The class-D `TraceEvent` tuple-struct declaration is not byte-exact-projectable

Resolved by the layout-4 tuple-field-visibility kernel slice. `core-logos`
`Newtype` gained a stored `wrapped_visibility: Visibility` (layout 3 → 4), so
`pub struct TraceEvent(pub ObjectName);` — a tuple newtype with a `pub` field — is
now modeled and projects byte-exact, and `textual-rust` reads and projects the
pub-field form. The class-D `TraceSupport` generator emits the `TraceEvent` struct
declaration in document order (between the `ObjectName` enum and `impl ObjectName`),
and the enriched byte-exact test covers it (44 items). The declaration is no longer
generated by the frozen schema-rust path.
